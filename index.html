About Me

Currently I am a Research Fellow at the National University of Singapore, working with Associate Prof. Bingsheng He. I received my Ph.D. degree from University of Science and Technology of China in 2016. My Advisor is Prof. Bei Hua. I was a visiting scholar at The Ohio State University from 2013 to 2015, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaodong Zhang. My research area includes Database System, Networked System, Parallel Computing, and Big Data.
