About Me

Dr. Kai Zhang is a Pre-tenure Associate Professor at Fudan University. He received his Ph.D. degree from University of Science and Technology of China in 2016. Before joining Fudan in Oct. 2017, he was a Research Fellow at National University of Singapore from Oct. 2016 to Oct. 2017. He was a Visiting Scholar at The Ohio State University from Sep. 2013 to Sep. 2015.

His research areas include Parallel and Distributed Computing, Database Systems, and Networked Systems.
